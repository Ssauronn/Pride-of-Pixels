BARRACKS

TIER 3 UPGRADES - AVAILABLE AT GAME CREATION
- OFFENSIVE 1a -Upgrades the range of Rangers.
- OFFENSIVE 1b -Upgrades the attack bonus of Berserker's enrage ability.
- OFFENSIVE 1c -Upgrades the Ambush damage of Rogues.
- DEFENSIVE 1 -	Upgrades the HP of Soldiers and Knights.
- SPECIAL 1a -	Unlocks the Berserker's Standard Ability, Enrage.
- SPECIAL 1b -	Unlocks the Rogue's Standard Ability, Invisibility.
- SPECIAL 1c -	Unlocks the Soldier's Standard Ability, Rally.
- SPECIAL 2 -	Unlocks the Special Ability for the basic unit chosen in your Combat
		Specialty skill.

TIER 2 UPGRADES
- OFFENSIVE 2a -Rogues are able to find the weakpoint in armor with their Ambush ability
		and now ignore half of the target's armor when attacking with Ambush.
- OFFENSIVE 2b -Further increases the range of Rangers.
- OFFENSIVE 2c -Increases the damage bonus for Soldiers when surrounded by other friendly
		Soldiers.
- DEFENSIVE 2a -Upgrades the magic armor of Knights.
- DEFENSIVE 2b -Slightly upgrades the slash and pierce armor of Berserkers.

TIER 1 UPGRADES
- SPECIAL 3 -	Ranger's arrows are now coated with magical fire, dealing small additional
		magical damage with each shot.
- OFFENSIVE 3 -	Increases the damage of all basic units.
- DEFENSIVE 3a -Increases the slash armor of all basic units.
- DEFENSIVE 3b -Increases the pierce armor of all basic units.
