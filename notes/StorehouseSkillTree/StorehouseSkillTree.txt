TIER 3 UPGRADES - AVAILABLE AT GAME CREATION
- INNOVATION 1a-Trained Workers: Increases the gathering speed of all base resources by Workers.
TECHNOLOGY UPGRADES
- INNOVATION 1b-Drones: Workers no longer need to drop base resources off at a Storehouse. 
		(Upgrade also available at Town Hall).

TIER 2 UPGRADES
- INNOVATION 2-	Advanced Tooling: Further increases the gathering speed of all base resources by Workers.
- SPECIAL 1 -	Drilling: Allows Workers to mine Rubies.
		(Upgrade also available at Town Hall).
- INNOVATION-3	Magic Training: Increases the gathering speed of Rubies by Workers.

TIER 1 UPGRADES
- INNOVATION 4-	Speedy Hands: Further increases the gathering speed of all base resources by Workers.
- SPECIAL 2a -	Roller Skates: Increases the movement speed of Workers while not in combat.
TECHNOLOGY UPGRADES
- SPECIAL 2b -	Futuristic Tooling: Workers are extremely efficient at resource gathering, and gain a 
		random amount of additional resources each time they gather from a resource node.
