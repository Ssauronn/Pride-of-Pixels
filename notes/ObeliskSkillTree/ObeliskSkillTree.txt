TIER 2 UPGRADES
- INNOVATION 1	Increases the sight range of Obelisks.
- SPECIAL 1 -	Obelisks now provide an armor bonus for all units and buildings within range.
MAGICAL UPGRADES
- CHOOSE TWO OF THE FOLLOWING
	- SPECIAL 2a -	Provides True Sight, revealing all Rogues and Subverters within range.
		- INNOVATION 2a	You retain True Sight on Rogues and Subverters revealed by your
		  Obelisks for a short time after they leave the Obelisk's range.
		- SPECIAL 3a -	All enemy units are slowed while in range of the Obelisk.
	- SPECIAL 2b -	Will summon a Demon when an enemy comes within range. Medium cooldown.
		- INNOVATION 2b	Demons summoned by the Obelisk have increased speed and attack
		  for a short time after being summoned.
		- SPECIAL 3b -	The Obelisk will summon a small number of Demons if it is
		  destroyed.
	- SPECIAL 2c -	An Obelisk can link to one unit. Once linked, if that unit would have
	  been killed, the unit is instead saved from death, set to 1 HP,
	  teleported to the Obelisk, and the Obelisk is then destroyed. An Obelisk
	  cannot be linked to a unit if it is not at full health.
		- INNOVATION 2c	The Obelisk is no longer destroyed when the linked unit would
		  have been killed and is instead set to 1 HP.
		- SPECIAL 3c -	Buildings within range of the Obelisk slowly heal over time.

TIER 1 UPGRADES
- INNOVATION 2d	Increases the effect range of Obelisks.
- SPECIAL 3d -	Obelisks now provide an attack bonus for all units and buildings within range.
- SPECIAL 4 -	You may now choose one sight target for each Obelisk. The sight target can only
  be another Obelisk. A narrow line of Vision is constantly provided between the
  original Obelisk and its sight target. Enemies can see your sight lines.
